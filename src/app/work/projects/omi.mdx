## Overview

Omi is the world's leading open-source AI wearable device that captures conversations in real time. It automatically transcribes meetings, conversations, and voice memos, then generates summaries, extracts action items, and performs actions on your behalf. Built the desktop app at Based Hardware with Nick Shevchenko. The hardware uses nRF and ESP32-S3 chips, with a Flutter mobile app, Python/FastAPI backend, and integrations with Firebase, Pinecone, Redis, and transcription services.
