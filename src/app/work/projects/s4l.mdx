## Overview

S4L (s4l.ai) is my current startup: a done-for-you targeted reply service that drives product growth on autopilot. Humans and AI agents hand-pick the high-intent Reddit and X threads where your buyers are already asking, write the reply in your brand voice, and post it from accounts that read as real community members. You pay only for signed-up users or scheduled B2B calls.

Under the hood it runs autonomous engagement pipelines across Reddit, Twitter/X, and GitHub, plus DM outreach, programmatic SEO page generation, and DataForSEO + Google Search Console integrations. It serves three audiences: solo founders and indie hackers who want the self-host open-source autoposter, established B2C brands that want fully managed buyer-community share of voice, and founders who want their personal brand on X ghostwritten in their own voice. The core autoposter is open-source at github.com/m13v/social-autoposter.
